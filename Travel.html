Travel



Portugal


  • I have been going to Portugal on holidays for the last 12 years. We head south to the Algarve. It is hugely popular destination due to it's fantastic beaches and great climate.
  • I have fond memories here and we are going to the Algarve again this Summer.
  • There are countless beautiful golf courses in the Algarve, we often go golfing and it is highly competitive!

Paris


  • I went to Paris on a school tour in 2016 for 3 days. It was my first time going to France and it was a great trip with my school friends.
  • We saw various tourist attractions like the Eiffel tower and Arc de Triomphe.
  • The highlight of the trip was most definitely watching PSG playing AS Monaco in a Ligue 1 game in the Le Parc des Princes.
  • The game ended 2-0 to Monaco, Kylian Mbappe played the majority of the game and was outstanding.
